08 — Board Summary
What worked, what broke, what's next
Key Takeaways
  • The multi-cap strategy partially worked. PM spend +18.6%, evening sales went from $84 to $4,831 (57× increase). But overall ad sales still fell –6.1% because the 12 PM dead zone wiped out the gains.
  • Evening is now a real revenue window. The 5 PM cap (Selva) unlocked $4,831 in sales that didn't exist before. Tuesday and Friday evenings were the strongest.
  • 12 PM is the new bottleneck. Budget exhausts by noon on Tue–Fri. W2 spent only $2,037 at 12 PM vs W1's $6,182 — a $12,037 loss in 12 PM sales alone.
  • Wednesday proved the concept. $18.5K spend, $53K sales, sustained PM activity 1–5 PM. When budget allocation matches demand, the multi-cap structure works end-to-end.
  • Friday was catastrophic. 5-hour PM dead zone (12–4 PM), only $33K in ad sales, lowest ROAS at 2.51x.
  • ROAS improved on Tue & Thu — the spend that DID happen was more efficient. The problem is insufficient total cap, not poor targeting.
⚡ Recommendation — Close the 12 PM Gap

Add a 12 PM cap (Selva) — NEW to prevent the noon dead zone. Move from 4 caps to 5:
